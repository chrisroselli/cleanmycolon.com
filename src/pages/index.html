Colon Hydrotherapy

Colon Hydrotherapy is an effective and safe way to achieve a clean colon. It has been practiced for centuries for its natural healing and restorative qualities. It is irrigation and “cleansing” of the rectum and large intestine through several safe, gentle infusions of temperature-varied, multi-filtered water. No chemicals or drugs are involved. My state-of-the-art FDA approved Dotolo Research equipment is unsurpassed in safety and hygiene. This closed system is both gentle and effective.
